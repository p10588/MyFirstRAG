Title: Company Leave Policy

Our company provides paid leave to all full-time employees. Employees are entitled to 14 days of annual leave, 7 days of sick leave, and additional special leave for urgent matters. Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 working days in advance except for emergency sick leaves. 

For parental leave, employees may apply for up to 12 weeks of unpaid leave under applicable local laws. Public holidays are not deducted from leave balances.

Title: Product Return Policy

Customers can return products within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, provided the items are in original condition with packaging intact. To initiate a return, customers must submit a return request form and receive an approval email.

Products damaged during shipping must be reported within 7 days. Refunds will be processed within 10 business days after the returned items are received.

Title: IT Security Guidelines

All employees must use company-provided devices with encrypted storage. Passwords must be at least 12 characters including upper case, lower case, digits, and symbols. Multi-factor authentication is required for all remote access.

Sharing login credentials is strictly prohibited. Suspicious emails should be reported immediately to the IT department.
